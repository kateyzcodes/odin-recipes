Irresistible Italian Corn

Description

This is a delicious seasoned corn side dish. DELICIOUS. Must TRY NOW!!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 tbsp butter
  • 2.5 cups whole kernel sweet corn
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over high heat.
  2. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely melted; stir in corn.
  3. Season corn with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Cook seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
